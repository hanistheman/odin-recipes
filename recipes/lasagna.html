Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a popular pasta dish from Italy. It consists of cheese, pasta, ground meat and sauce, and is baked into a good finishing product. Recommended to eat with garlic bread or a leafy green salad.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lbs ground beef
  • Diced onion
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can tomato sauce
  • 2 tbsp chopped parsley
  • 1 clove garlic
  • A dash of sugar (1/8 tbsp)
  • Dried basil
  • Dried oregano
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Shredded mozarella
  • Eggs

Directions

  1. Cook ground beef until browned. Add in onions next, followed with canned tomato products, 1/2 of the parsley, garlic, basil, 1.5 tsp of salt, oregano and sugar.
  2. Cook the pasta noodles in boiled water until al dente.
  3. Assemble the cheese layer by mixing ricotta, parmesan, eggs, the remaining parsley, salt and pepper.
  4. Assemble the lasagna and layer accordingly, starting with salt and ending with mozzarella.
  5. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F. Do this before placing the lasagna.
  6. Cover with foil and bake in oven for 1/2 an hour. Remove the foil and continue baking until the top is golden brown.
