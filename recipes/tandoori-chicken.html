Tandoori Chicken

Description

Tandoori Chicken is a popular chicken dish from India. It is very spicy, but also very flavorful. This recipe can be cooked on a grill in lieu of a clay oven. Best if eaten with basmati rice, corn on the cob, grilled veggies, or a cucumber salad.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 1 1/4 cups plain yogurt
  • 1/2 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp grated fresh ginger root
  • 1 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 2 tsp finely chopped cilantro
  • 1 medium lemon, cut into wedges

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Remove and discard skin from chicken. Cut slits into meat and place in shallow dish. Season chicken on both sides with lemon juice and salt. Let sit for 20 minutes.
  3. Mix onion, yogurt, garlic, garam masala, ginger and cayenne pepper together in a medium bowl until smooth.
  4. Spread yogurt mixture over chicken, cover and refrigerate for 6-24 hours.
  5. When ready to cook, preheat an outdoor gril for medium high heat and lightly oil the grate. Remove chicken from the marinade and discard leftover marinade.
  6. Cook chicken on grill until no longer pink and juices run clear.
  7. Garnish with cilantro and lemon wedges and serve.
